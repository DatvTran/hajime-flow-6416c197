## Design Context

### Users
Hajime is a B2B operations app used by multiple roles (Hajime HQ brand operators, manufacturers, distributors/sales reps, and retail) to coordinate inventory, orders, shipments, and related supply/commercial operations.

### Brand Personality
Clean, clinical, and composed. The UI should feel reliable, calm, and “uncluttered,” emphasizing legibility and precision over ornament.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light-first product UI with optional dark mode later. Prioritize: high contrast hierarchy, restrained decoration, consistent spacing, and crisp component states.

### Quality Bar
Flagship polish: small-but-noticeable refinements to spacing, typography, and interaction states across shared UI primitives and common layouts without introducing functional risk.

### Design Principles
- Prefer clarity over cleverness: remove visual noise, reduce competing accents.
- Make hierarchy obvious: fewer text sizes, stronger weight/contrast separation.
- Interactions must feel “engineered”: consistent focus, hover/active/disabled/loading states.
- Consistency beats novelty: use tokens and shared primitives; no one-off styling.
